Notes

The sizes of margins and paddings using the p-$ and m-$ follow the relation below:

m-$ Equivalent
0 0rem
1 0.25rem
2 0.5rem
3 1rem
4 1.5rem
5 3rem

The default bottom margin is 3rem or m-5.

The margins from two elements(?) will try to overlap, this means that two m-4 paragraphs that are touching will be separated by 1.5rem instead of summing the two margins.

px and py can be used to access the padding-inline and padding-block use cases.

Margins and paddings can vary with breakpoints, as in p-sm-5.

mx-auto can be used to centralize a div within another div.
