a tag defer indica que o arquivo de script será carregado apenas depois do carregamento dos outros elementos na página, equivale a colocar a tag script no final da página

I used <div class="row align-items-xxl-center"> to center vertically the items in the row when the page reaches the xxl breakpoint and <div class="row justify-content-center"> to center the columns

useful variations of <div class="row justify-content-[variations]"> other than start, end, and center are between, around, and evenly

I used the attribute offset-1

I used the attribute ms-auto and mx-auto

I think ms, me, mx, my are margin-start, margin-end, the horizontal margin, and the vertical margin
