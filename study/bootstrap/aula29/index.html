Notes

carousel-control-next e carousel-control-prev control where the button will be positioned, while carousel-control-prev-icon e carousel-control-next-icon control what icon will be shown.

I accidentaly found out that putting the carousel-caption inside the carousel-item makes the caption slide with the item, while putting it outside makes it static and the next item slides above it.

Doing it on the last item makes it stay above the carousel at all the times. This will be ver useful to the project.

Adding a data-bs-touch="false" to the carousel will disable the touch sliding for mobile users.
